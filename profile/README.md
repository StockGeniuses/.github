# StockGeniuses

**A decision workspace for stock investors.**

StockGeniuses is building an AI-assisted stock research and decision workspace for self-directed investors.

It brings stock discovery, structured company analysis, investor-inspired frameworks, watchlists, portfolios, signals, research notes, and decision journaling into one connected workflow.

**Discover → Analyze → Decide → Track → Reflect → Improve**

## What we believe

- Good investing decisions require structure, not more noise.
- AI should explain evidence rather than replace human judgment.
- Research should preserve the reasoning behind every decision.
- A repeatable process matters more than confident predictions.
- Investors should be able to revisit what they believed, why they believed it, and what changed.

## What you will find here

This organization hosts selected public educational resources created by StockGeniuses.

These resources may include:

- Stock research checklists
- Company-analysis frameworks
- Investment thesis templates
- Risk and invalidation templates
- Decision journals
- Thesis-review workflows
- Decision post-mortems
- Educational references and research guides

Our public repositories are intended to help self-directed investors build a clearer, more structured, and more reflective research process.

The StockGeniuses production application, internal scoring systems, proprietary models, prompts, infrastructure, and private product repositories are not published here.

## Our approach to AI

StockGeniuses uses AI as an interpreter, not an authority.

AI can help explain financial information, organize evidence, summarize context, and clarify tradeoffs. It should not replace the investor’s judgment or issue personalized commands.

**AI explains. You decide.**
